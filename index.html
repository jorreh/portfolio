Maison Mathis - Prospect Zone

Maison Mathis is a Food & Beverage concept created by Creneau International, which evolves around the traditional Belgian food culture. The Maison Mathis Prospect Zone is a digital web-based platform that I created during my internship at Creneau (2014). This platform contains an overview of all documents, media and information that is related to the Maison Mathis concept, and is used as a sales tool by Creneau. The Maison Mathis platform helps potential clients get an idea of how the Maison Mathis Franchise is structured, and the steps that will be necessary to launch a Maison Mathis restaurant themselves.

Belgian Beer Caf√© - Audit Forms

Belgian Beer Cafe is a worldwide bar/restaurant concept created by Creneau International. During my internship at Creneau I developed a web-based platform to execute, manage and centralize audit forms for the Belgian Beer Cafe franchise. Audit forms are filled in by an 'auditor': a person that is responsible for trademark protection, quality control and supervising the abidance to the franchise concepts by concept owners. These audit forms used to be printed out on paper and were collected in all different places, which resulted in an unorganized whole. Through the creation of a web-based platform for these audit forms, with a centralized database, I managed to bring some order to the chaos.

XBag

xBag is an interactive audio installation using the Axoloti platform. Along with 3 interaction students I created something that could be described as an 'augmented instrument'. An augmented instrument is a traditional instrument (in this case an electric guitar) with added functionality. By adding 2 different sensors to the guitar we could alter the sound of the guitar by either moving the guitar neck up and down, or by pushing a touch strip sensor attached to the back of the guitar neck. For all the sound modulations we made use of Axoloti (an electronic module combined with a software platform for audio synthesis, developed by Johannes Taelman, a Belgian audio engeneer and researcher).

Patient X

Patient x is an interactive, audiovisual game. This game was developed in the context of a 3-month school module called 'Play&Game'. In this game two players get to take place behind a computer, each in a different room. They are both hooked up to a heartrate monitor. The clue of the game is that the two players do not know they are playing against each other. In this game, each player is the other player's self-delusion, and they have to eliminate this delusion, by trying to keep their heartrate down, and by taking some (fictional) experimental drugs. The person who is able to scare his own delusion away, and keep his own heartrate down wins the game.

Intercellar

Intercellar is an interactive sound installation, designed as a school project during a module called 'Stories'. The installation is located in a dark, cellar-like room. The participant gets to wander around the room with headphones, through which audio samples are played. The audio samples are all short fragments of a story, and are triggered by the position of the participant in the room. By walking around, the participant starts to unravel the event that took place inside the cellar.

Saint-Gilles Popup Store

Popup Store Saint-Gilles is web-concept and design for a fictional popup store in Saint-Gilles, Liege. This was a school project, commissioned by the movement of Saint Gilles. This website offers a tool for the citizens, to make them capable of undertaking something by themselves and start up their own unique pop up shop. The website also offers them more information: a checklist with all the different formal steps they need to complete before they can open their pop up shop.

Oersoep

Oersoep is an interactive animation created in Processing. It makes use of the the simpleFluids library for Processing. In this application, microorganisms (amoebas) swim around on the screen, as if looked trough a microscope. By stirring around this 'oersoep' with the mouse you can move these creatures around, or even push them out of the screen.

About

Hi! My name is Jorre. I'm a 25 year old multimedia artist, graduated from the Luca School of Arts. I specialized in Interaction Design. Combining my technical skills with my passion for design, I create websites, audiovisual installations and animations. One of my favorite tools for interactive designs is Processing.
